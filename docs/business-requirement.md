# Business Requirements Document

## Project Name
Enterprise Employee Onboarding Platform

## Business Problem

The organization currently manages employee onboarding through emails, spreadsheets, and manual processes. This causes delays, missing information, and limited visibility into onboarding status.

## Business Goals

- Automate onboarding processes
- Reduce manual effort
- Improve employee experience
- Increase reporting visibility
- Standardize onboarding workflows

## Stakeholders

- Human Resources
- Hiring Managers
- IT Department
- New Employees
- Executive Leadership

## Success Metrics

- 50% reduction in onboarding time
- 90% workflow automation
- Improved onboarding completion rates
- Real-time reporting through Power BI
